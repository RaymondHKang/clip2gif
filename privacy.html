Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

Clip2GIF ("we", "our", or "the site") is a free browser-based tool that converts video clips to GIFs. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Video files stay on your device

All video processing happens locally in your web browser. We do not upload, store, or transmit your video files to our servers. Once you close the page, your files are gone from the app.

Information we may collect

  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics or similar services to understand how visitors use the site (pages viewed, device type, approximate location). This helps us improve the product.
  • Advertising: We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your visits to this and other websites. See Google's advertising policy for details and opt-out options.
  • Hosting logs: Our hosting provider (e.g. Cloudflare, Vercel) may log standard request data such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps for security and performance.

Cookies

Third-party ad and analytics providers may set cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Where required by law, we will display a cookie consent banner before non-essential cookies are set.

Children's privacy

Clip2GIF is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
